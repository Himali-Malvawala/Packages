Add optional `avatar`, `breadcrumbs`, and `chips` slots to `PageHeader`. `avatar` renders in place of the boxed `icon` (for a person/entity photo), `breadcrumbs` renders a trail above the title row, and `chips` renders status pills next to the title. All are optional and backward-compatible — existing `icon`/`title`/`subtitle` usage is unchanged.
